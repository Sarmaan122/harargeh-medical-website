About Harargeh Medical

Learn about our history, mission, and commitment to healthcare excellence

Our History

Founded in 2003, Harargeh Medical and Surgical Centre began as a small clinic with a vision to provide accessible, quality healthcare to the Harar community. Over the years, we have grown into a full-fledged medical center with multiple specialties.

Our commitment to excellence has earned us recognition as one of the leading healthcare providers in the region. We continuously invest in advanced medical technology and training for our staff to ensure we deliver world-class care.

Our Mission

To provide comprehensive, compassionate, and accessible healthcare services that improve the health and well-being of our community through clinical excellence, innovative technology, and a patient-centered approach.

Our Vision

To be the leading healthcare institution in Eastern Ethiopia, recognized for exceptional patient care, medical education, and community health initiatives.

Our Values

Compassion

Treating every patient with empathy, dignity, and respect

Excellence

Pursuing the highest standards in medical care

Integrity

Upholding ethical principles in all our actions

Collaboration

Working together for the best patient outcomes

Our Medical Services

Comprehensive healthcare services for all your medical needs

Cardiology

Our cardiology department offers advanced diagnostic and treatment services for heart conditions including echocardiography, stress tests, cardiac catheterization, and interventional cardiology procedures.

  • Cardiac Consultation
  • Echocardiography
  • Stress Testing
  • Angioplasty & Stenting

Neurology

Our neurology department provides comprehensive care for disorders of the brain, spine, and nervous system including epilepsy, stroke, Parkinson's disease, and multiple sclerosis.

  • Neurological Consultation
  • EEG & EMG Testing
  • Stroke Management
  • Headache Clinic

Pediatrics

Our pediatric department provides specialized healthcare for infants, children, and adolescents in a child-friendly environment with services ranging from routine checkups to specialized care.

  • Well-child Visits
  • Vaccinations
  • Developmental Screening
  • Pediatric Emergency Care

Radiology

Our radiology department provides state-of-the-art imaging services including X-ray, ultrasound, CT scan, and MRI to support accurate diagnosis and treatment planning.

  • Digital X-ray
  • Ultrasound Scanning
  • CT Scan
  • MRI Services

General Surgery

Our surgical department offers a wide range of surgical procedures including minimally invasive surgeries, laparoscopic procedures, and traditional surgeries with modern techniques.

  • Laparoscopic Surgery
  • Gastrointestinal Surgery
  • Trauma Surgery
  • Minor Surgical Procedures

Ophthalmology

Our ophthalmology department provides comprehensive eye care including vision testing, cataract surgery, glaucoma treatment, and correction of refractive errors.

  • Eye Examinations
  • Cataract Surgery
  • Glaucoma Management
  • LASIK & Refractive Surgery
